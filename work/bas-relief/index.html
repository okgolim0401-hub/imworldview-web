As a sequel to <TokTok Shadow>, this artwork deconstructs the formative language of individual lighting fixtures designed by 16 designers, realizing them into a single bas-relief. The functional objects, which originally illuminated spaces and emitted light, have had their innate utility eliminated, reassembled while retaining only their forms and structures.

Inheriting the SLS nylon 3D printing technique - the core identity of <TokTok Shadow> - we realized the detailed forms of individual objects. Simultaneously, to dimensionally secure the massive mass characteristic of the large scale, machined foam material was integrated as a base. Through this structural fusion of high-precision 3D printed parts and the large mass, we expressed the heavy sense of volume and delicate textures unique to bas-relief.
